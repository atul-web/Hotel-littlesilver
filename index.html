About Us

Imagine waking up to the sound of birds chirping and the smell of fresh pine trees. You open curtains to a breathtaking view of the Fairy Forest, a lush green oasis in the heart of the Kashmir Valley. This is a Little Silver Hotel, a paradise on earth.

We are located in Pahalgam, Kashmir. Kashmir is a home to snow-capped mountains, lush green valleys, and crystal-clear lakes. It’s a place where you can hike, bike, camp, or simply relax and enjoy the scenery.

And now, with Little Silver Hotel, you can experience all that Kashmir has to offer in comfort and style. This beautiful hotel is near Aru Valley in Pahalgam and it offers everything you need for a luxurious Vacation. Your Spacious room will have a comfortable bed, a TV, Free Wi-fi, and 24/7 room service. And the hotel’s restaurant serves delicious Kashmir cuisine, with something for everyone from vegetarians to meat-lovers.

Our Restaurant : with Kashmiri Specialities

Kashmiri cuisine is majorly known for its use of saffron, which gives dishes a distinctive golden colour and aroma. Other Spices commonly used in Kashmir cuisine include cardamon, cinnamon, cloves, ginger, and turmeric. Our restaurant offers a wide variety of Kashmiri delicacies that will transport you to a different world. We also have a wide range of other dishes for you, from morning breakfast to evening Dinner

If you’re a Non-vegetarian, you’ll love our lamb curry. It’s made with fresh lamb, saffron, and a blend of spices that will make your mouth water. We also have a variety of other non-vegetarian dishes, such as tabak maaz (lamb ribs), goshtaba (minced Lamb), and yakhni (lamb Stew)

No Matter what your dietary preferences are, you're sure to find something to your taste at our restaurant. We use only the freshest ingredients and cook everything to order, so you can be sure that your food will be delicious
